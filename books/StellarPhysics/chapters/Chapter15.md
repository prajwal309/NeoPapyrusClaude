# Chapter 15 — Stellar Rotation and Magnetism

---

## 15.1 Rotation of Stars

<div data-level="101">

Stars spin.

Some slowly, some very fast.

</div>

---

<div data-level="201">

Rotation evolves due to:

- Magnetic braking  
- Angular momentum loss  

</div>

---

## 15.2 Dynamo Processes

<div data-level="201">

Magnetic fields are generated by:

- Convection  
- Rotation  

</div>

---

## 15.3 Magnetic Cycles

<div data-level="201">

Stars show cycles:

- Like the Sun’s 11-year cycle  

</div>

---

## 15.4 Activity–Rotation Relation

<div data-level="301">

Faster rotation → stronger magnetic activity  

Key relation:

- Activity correlates with Rossby number  

</div>

---

## Summary

- Rotation and magnetism are linked  
- Magnetic fields drive activity  
- Rotation evolves over time
