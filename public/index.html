About NeoPapyrus

This design moves away from rectangular cards and toward a more iconic visual language: circular medallions, luminous paths, and a central knowledge core. Each book uses its own cover art as the node background, so the tree feels less like a sitemap and more like a world map of disciplines.

The result is closer to an Assassin’s Creed skill tree: ritual, symmetric, atmospheric, and unlock-like. The structure still comes from your book graph, but the experience feels more legendary and less utilitarian.

Design Notes

Each node can have a cover image, subtitle, and recommendation badge. You can later add states like locked, completed, featured, or new.

For the best effect, make all cover images similar in aspect ratio and store them consistently as /books/BookSlug/coverPage/frontpage.png.
